Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

Purefusion TorrentBridge is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe that your browsing activity and download habits are your own.

Commitment: This extension does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to the developer or any third parties.

How It Works

All functionality is executed locally within your browser context:

  • Configuration: Your server details (IP, login) are stored in your local browser storage (Chrome Sync) and are never sent to us.
  • Communication: When you click a link, the extension communicates directly from your browser to your configured BitTorrent client. This traffic does not pass through any intermediate servers.

Permissions

Permission Purpose
Hosts (http/https) Required to send API requests to your own BitTorrent client.
All URLs Used only to scan for .torrent/magnet: interactions on pages you visit.
