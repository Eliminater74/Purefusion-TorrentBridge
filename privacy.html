Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

Purefusion TorrentBridge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. All functionality is handled locally within the user's browser. No external servers or third-party services are contacted.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect or track:

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Browsing history
  • Web activity or usage data
  • Cookies or session data

Permissions

Permissions are used solely for providing features, such as context menus or interacting with the BitTorrent web client. These permissions are not used to access or transmit data.
